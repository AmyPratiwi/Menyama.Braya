Balinese Awig-Krama

Respect & preserve local traditions by understanding tourism ethics in Bali.

1

Maintaining Temple Sanctity

Avoid entering sacred temple areas during menstruation, with open bleeding wounds, or during a period of mourning. Wear Balinese traditional kamen wrap-around cloth.

2

Respecting Offerings (Canang Sari)

Do not step over or step on canang sari offerings placed on roads or entrances. If accidentally touched, offer a silent prayer of apology.

3

Respectful Dress & Behavior

Wear modest, polite clothing when moving around local neighborhood areas or sacred spaces. Maintain sweet, polite, and respectful speech.

4

Sacred Area Photography Limits

Always ask for permission from your local host or residents before photographing traditional ceremonies or inner temple sanctums.

"By understanding Balinese customs, you actively support the preservation of the sacred Bali Taksu."
